WI tracked a high number of subscription issues and an increasing cost from call center during the time we tried to reshape up the future of global selling services. The goal for this project was to understand the reasons behind of the subscription issues and improve our billing experience by reassuring the global sellers that we would provide an intuitive subscription and billing process.

Design Brief


Problem Statement

After identifying customer journey and persona I concluded a design brief for my design thinking and validation.

  • All users only can request subscription & billing service from call center. And it took some business days to response.
  • There's no clear message to notify them on the current subscription plans, invoice status, upgrade or cancellation.

hypotheses

My hypothesis was that if the users could easily manage invoices, subscriptions and billing services by using one-stop checkout UI in WI seller portal, we would not only reduce customer tickets and cost at call center, but also speed up customer services.

Success Metrics

After agreed with stockholders, they are our success metrics:

  • Reduced number of customer requests by 50%.
  • 80% of users are able to run services on their own.
  • UI can be easily adoptable to the existing Seller portal.

Existing subscription & billing services have been taken into account to create a new experience flow.

Solution


Over the discovery and research stage, I explored ideas by sketching as many as possible and turned the best concept into a series of mockups. I then validated the mockups internally across the business as WI could give valuable insights. After some iterations and tweaking based on their feedback, I created a final prototype which I validated on WI customers.

Here is what I concluded for the final design solution:

Most of customers are new to WI service and the only way to complete a process of subscription and billing was calling to customer service, as resulted in high number of customer requests and cost. By building a one-stop checkout process from subscription to billing that provides a way to let customers manage their needs by themselves in a central place.

  • Provide a self-help subscription and billing process
  • Provide ways to notify the type of subscriptions they've purchased or canceled and upgraded.
